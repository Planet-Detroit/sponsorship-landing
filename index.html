Join the organizations building a more sustainable Metro Detroit

Planet Detroit's partners are the businesses, institutions, and nonprofits powering a healthier, more resilient region. When you partner with us, you stand alongside the organizations doing this work — and connect with the community that cares most about it.

Why Partner

Your partnership sustains the journalism — and puts you in the room

Planet Detroit partners don't just support independent environmental journalism. They join a visible community of organizations committed to sustainability, public health, and equity across Metro Detroit. Two partnership tiers offer different levels of visibility and engagement — but every partner helps keep our coverage free, accessible, and independent.

Flagship

Planet Champions

$2,500 / year

For the organizations at the forefront of sustainability, resilience, and community impact in Metro Detroit. Planet Champions receive a professionally produced Q&A profile published on Planet Detroit — and year-round recognition as part of the cohort driving the region forward.

What's included

  • Planet Champion Q&A — a published narrative feature about your organization's work and vision
  • LinkedIn promotion of your profile to Planet Detroit's professional network
  • Dedicated newsletter feature spotlighting your profile
  • Homepage placement for your feature
  • Listing on Planet Detroit's website
  • Year-round recognition in our newsletter and on social media
  • Quarterly insider briefing with editorial updates
  • Advisory board meeting invitations
  • Event and job posting access
  • Event partnership — co-present one of Planet Detroit's ongoing community events, or work with our team to design a custom gathering for your audience. We handle promotion, guest curation, and editorial coverage. Custom events are scoped and priced separately.
